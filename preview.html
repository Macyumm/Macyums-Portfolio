About Me

Hey! I’m a 3D modeler who likes to live in both worlds—the sharp, clean lines of low poly and the intricate detail of high poly. Whether it’s for Roblox, games, or other projects, I turn ideas into models that actually feel alive. I love experimenting with style, scale, and personality in every mesh, and I’m always up for a challenge: from tiny props to full environments, if you can imagine it, I can probably model it. When I’m not tweaking vertices or perfecting topology, I’m thinking about how to make your virtual world a little more fun, quirky, or just plain cool.
